**Karpathy's Approach**
----------------------

### Overview

Karpathy's approach to LLM knowledge bases emphasizes the importance of Markdown as a source of truth and the role of self-evolving systems in improving over time.

### Key Points

* **Markdown as Source of Truth**: Karpathy suggests using Markdown as the primary source of truth for LLMs, implying that natural language processing tasks can be simplified by leveraging existing documentation.
* **LLM as Research Librarian**: The author posits that an LLM can act as a research librarian, providing access to knowledge and improving its performance over time through self-improvement.
* **Self-Evolving Systems**: Karpathy's approach highlights the potential of self-evolving systems to improve over time, suggesting that this property is crucial for the success of LLMs.

**Key Concepts**
----------------

### No Vector DB Needed for Small-Medium Collections

Karpathy's approach suggests that no vector database is necessary for small- to medium-sized collections, implying that other methods may be more efficient or effective.

### Context > Retrieval

The author emphasizes the importance of context in natural language processing tasks, suggesting that retrieval is a key consideration in LLM design.

### LLM = CPU, Context = RAM

Karpathy's approach compares the role of an LLM to that of a CPU and context to that of RAM, highlighting the distinction between computational resources and contextual information.
