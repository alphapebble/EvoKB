### What We Collect

#### You Provide
Name, email, phone, company, messages
#### Auto-Collected
IP, browser, device, pages visited, cookies
We collect only what's necessary.

### How We Use Data

#### Service Delivery
Provide, maintain, improve services (contractual necessity)
#### Communication
Support, newsletters with consent (withdrawable)
#### Analytics & Security
Usage monitoring, fraud prevention (legitimate interests)
#### Legal
Compliance, enforcing terms (legal obligation)

We won't use data incompatibly without fresh consent (DPDP Sec. 5, GDPR Art. 5).

### Sharing & Security

#### We Share With
Service providers (cloud, analytics), legal authorities when required, business transfers (mergers)
#### Security
SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, regular audits

### Retention
Only as long as necessary or required by law

### Your Rights

All Users:
• Access, correct, delete your data
• Withdraw consent anytime
• Data portability
• Object to processing
India (DPDP):
Nominate representative, grievance redressal
EU (GDPR):
Right to be forgotten
Contact:
`privacy@alphapebble.io` (respond in 30 days)

### International & Children

#### Transfers
GDPR uses Standard Contractual Clauses. DPDP follows Government of India guidelines.
#### Children
Not for under-13 globally, under-18 in India without parental consent. We delete children's data if discovered.

### Updates
30 days' notice for material changes.

### Questions?
Email `privacy@alphapebble.io`

**Key Claims and Evidence:**

* Alpha Pebble collects only necessary information from users.
* The company uses data for legitimate interests, such as service delivery and analytics.
* Users have the right to access, correct, and delete their data, as well as withdraw consent at any time.
* Alpha Pebble complies with data protection regulations in India (DPDP) and the EU (GDPR).
* The company has implemented measures to ensure security and sharing of personal information.
